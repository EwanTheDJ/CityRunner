The Introduction

Welcome to the newest development by Edge Games!

CityRunner is a brand new game made for people who enjoy a fast paced challenge!

It's a game thats all about timing! Collect as much treasure hidden around the city and escape in time or be stuck in the city forever!

The Details

So, how has development been?

Read our takes below:

Sound Design

The sound in the game has been sourced for free from Freesound .

Version Control

Version control has been as easy as pie thanks to Github, providing a place to upload branches and merge to main once features have been completed.

Menu Development

The Menus all flow between each other flawlessly, from the main menu you can reach the how to play menu, each with their own backing music.

Asset sourcing

The player model and animations were sourced from Mixamo allowing us to easily animate the character we were using. The environment was sourced for free from Sketchfab .

The Team and engine of choice

This game has been Indie led. Only one developer, made on the famous Unity Engine!

Can we see a demo soon?

Sooner than you think! A demo is ready and will be released to the public in the coming months.
